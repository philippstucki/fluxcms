DEPRECATED use xslt transformer instead!

Transforms an XML-Document with the help of libxslt out of domxml

libxslt is the Gnome XSLT-Processor from the libxml project. It's integrated in ext/domxml since 4.2 and has the big advantage that it takes a DomDocument Object for processing. Furthermore it's said to be much faster than sablotron.
